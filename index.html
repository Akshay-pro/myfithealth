About MyFitPrint

MyFitPrint is a scientifically developed platform to assess your overall wellness. It is powered by deep physiology intelligence built over years of scientific research. The technology is developed at MetFlux, a startup from IIT Bombay. MyFitPrint helps to analyze your health and risks with your minimum efforts and resources from your side. During your health assessment, the app asks you questions about your basic anthropometry, diet, activity, lifestyle, and diagnostics and generates a comprehensive health map of you. MyFitPrint also sets your fitness goals and provides personalized diet, activity, and lifestyle advice to achieve your fitness goals. With MyFitPrint, our mission is to empower you to assess your fitness in a scientific way before you act and transform your health.

Who is MyFitPrint for

Individuals

Parents

FAQs

MyFitPrint is a self-health assessment platform for adults and children. The platform is powered by MetFlux Deep Physiology Engine built over years of scientific research. The app asks you a set of questions related to basic anthropometry, work & leisure activity, diet, and provides you scientific analysis based on your answers. The output is divided into different sections and quickly provides you related analysis for free after answering a few related questions. You can receive a downloadable MyFitPrint Premium report after answering a complete set of questions. MyFitPrint Premium report includes body fitness, diet, activity, disease risks and lifestyle scores analysis. MyFitPrint also sets your health goals and gives scientifically curated diet, activity, and lifestyle advice to achieve your fitness goals.
To generate MyFitPrint report accurately, users should answer the questions accurately. The diagnostics section in the report is optional, but to predict the disease risks with better accuracy, we expect you to submit relevant diagnostics data. By design, MyFitPrint predicts the disease risks based on your answers to diet, activity, and lifestyle questions along with diagnostic data. In the absence of diagnostic data, the other parameters are used to calculate risks and scores, which are based on our scientific research over the years and are validated extensively.
Data privacy and protection is important to us. We take utmost care while handling your data. MyFitPrint follows Digital Information Security in Healthcare Act (DISHA) guidelines to protect your health data. We use state-of-the-art data security infrastructure to protect your data.
